About me

I currently work as a Help Desk Team Lead, responsible for a small team overseeing customer service within our company.

Often working closely with our development team in the diagnosis of bug fixes, I know how valuable and time-restrictive this can be, and to also work within SLAs.

The reason for wanting to switch my career is simple. I enjoy writing code.

The constant learning, sense of fulfilment and collaborative nature makes it a career that I amm willing to fight for. What I may lack in professional experience, I make up for in my determination to succeed.

Away from work, I am a keen musician. I play several instruments, including:

  • Bass
  • Drums

I have been in numerous rock bands throughout the years, recording and releasing a few pieces of music (see the video for a taste!). Although I no longer play live, I am now more focused on the production side as a mix engineer in my spare time.
